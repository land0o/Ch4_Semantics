Marsha M. Blackburn

Blackburn is the junior senator from Tennessee and is a Republican. She has served since Jan 3, 2019. Blackburn is next up for reelection in 2024

She was previously the representative for Tennessee’s 7th congressional district as a Republican from 2003 to 2018.
